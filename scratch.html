Becoming a True Warrior

-Three Reasons Why You Should Watch Vinland Saga-

  1. It teaches the importance of caring for others
    • Vinland Saga is the life story of Thorfinn, son of Thors. As a child, he joins his father on a trip to war, and what happens next changes his life for the worst. Though it might seem like a story focused on revenge and violence, it teaches us that humans should not fight one another. Vinland Saga teaches that we have no enemies.

  2. It shows the powerful message of personal growth

  3. It is action-packed, and action is America's favorite movie genre
